Why Open Source?

Design systems should belong to everyone. Here's why we're free forever.

Freedom & Ownership

  • No vendor lock-in. Modify, extend, or fork without restrictions.
  • MIT Licensed. Use commercially, privately, or anywhere you want.
  • No paywalls. Every component, token, and feature is free forever.
  • Your code, your control. Self-host, customize, or build upon it.

Community & Transparency

  • Built in public. Every decision, every line of code is visible on GitHub.
  • Community contributions. Your ideas and PRs shape the future of Rukn.
  • No tracking or analytics. Your privacy is respected, always.
  • Learn and grow. Study the code, understand the patterns, make it yours.
